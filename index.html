Dzagli & Co

Case Study

Dzagli & Co is a Ghanain based car manufacturing company. The company struggles to effeciently track and manage some of their processes. This leads to bad communication, bad organization and inconsistent processes. Dzagli & Co needs a way so that the supervisors can track and manage the operations of the company.

User Story

As a Supervisor at Dzagli & Co I would like a way to track and manage the inventory, the workers, the storage area, the packing areas, and the vehicles. So that I can hold the employees accountable when necessary, and improve their efficiency

Functionalities

1. The supervisor should be able to track and manage the makes designed for a given car.
2. The supervisor should be able to track and manage the location of various car parts.
3. The supervisor should be able to track and manage the location of the various vehicles.
